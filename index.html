AI-Empowered Visual Experiences

November 14, 2024 (Beijing time, morning) or November 13, 2024 (Seattle time, afternoon)

Introduction

Generative AI technologies are rapidly transforming how visual experiences are crafted and consumed, enabling a new level of creativity, personalization, and efficiency for users. In this workshop, we delve into the latest AI-driven advancements reshaping design, multimodal understanding, and human-computer interaction. Beginning with a keynote on the role of AI in creative tools, we will explore state-of-the-art applications in graphical design, such as Microsoft 365 Copilotâ€™s create module and next-gen business content generation models, which streamline and enhance the design process. The sessions will then highlight recent breakthroughs in multimodal foundation models, covering innovations in visual, audio, and video understanding. Finally, our HCI discussions showcase AI's potential to enrich human interaction through lifelike, real-time talking faces and advanced touchscreen hand-tracking for remote collaboration. Join us to discover how generative AI is revolutionizing visual experiences across fields.
